The Cake Cutting Contest

Upload a bot
Run a contest

It’s going to be my birthday in just a couple of weeks and there’s going to be cake.
One cake. And I’m going to share it.
Fair person that I am, I’m letting you guys decide how much cake you want.
However, I know you’ll be greedy and try to take as much as you can.
To solve this I’ve come up with an amazing system for fairly cutting the cake.
The bot who bids the least gets that amount of cake.

  • Each bot will bid an amount of cake
  • The bot who bids for the least amount of cake gets that much
  • They are now out of the running and have their cake.
  • The remaining bots repeat this process until either:
    • There are no more bots
    • There is no more cake
  • Bots may be written in the following languages:
    • awk
    • bash
    • c++
    • java
    • node.js
    • python 2
    • python 3
    • R
    • ruby
    • sh
  • Other languages may be added upon request
  • Bots may not use file storage
  • Bots will take input through command line arguments
  • Bots will output an integer through stdout
